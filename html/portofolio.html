Our Commercial and Industrial Projects

Greenray has been trusted to carry out more than 150 solar power plant (PLTS) projects in Indonesia.

Solar Panel Installation Project

Residential Project – Surabaya Home

  • Capacity: 5 kWp
  • Goal: Reduce household electricity bills.
  • Details: Estimated electricity savings up to 60% per month. Integrated system with mobile monitoring application.

Commercial Project – Bandung Retail Store

  • Capacity: 20 kWp
  • Goal: Reduce store operational costs.
  • Details: Estimated electricity bill reduction of Rp 7 million per month. Panels installed on a 3-story building, connected to the PLN grid system.

Industrial Project – Textile Factory Semarang

  • Capacity: 200 kWp
  • Objective: Supporting the green industry program
  • Result: Reducing carbon emissions by 120 tons/year
  • Details: On-grid system with high-capacity industrial inverter.

Educational Institution Project – Jakarta University

  • Capacity: 50 kWp
  • Goal: Support a green campus concept and operational cost savings.
  • Details: Estimated electricity savings of Rp 15 million per month. Panels installed on laboratory and library buildings.

Government Project – Village Office in Yogyakarta

  • Capacity: 10 kWp
  • Objective: Achieving an energy-independent village
  • Result: Meeting electricity needs of the village office & public street lighting.
  • Details: Hybrid system with 40 kWh energy storage battery.

Tourism Project – Bali Resort

  • Capacity: 30 kWp
  • Goal: Reduce resort electricity costs and support the eco-tourism concept.
  • Details: Most lighting needs met by solar power. Hybrid system with 60 kWh battery ensures operations continue at night or cloudy weather.
Powering a cleaner, brighter future for Indonesia. We are your trusted partner in sustainable energy solutions, built on transparency and long-term value.
