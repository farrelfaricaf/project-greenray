Reduce household electricity bills

Project Overview

Result

Successfully reduced bills by 60%

Details

Grid system with module monitoring unit

Energy Generation

Generates approximately 4,500 kWh annually, covering 60% of the household's energy needs. The system includes net metering, allowing excess energy to be sold back to the grid.

Project Gallery

Challenges & Solutions

Challenges

  • Limited roof space due to roof vents and chimneys
  • Shading from nearby trees during afternoon
  • Old roof structure requiring reinforcement

Solutions

  • Optimized panel layout to maximize available space
  • Installed micro-inverters to minimize shading impact
  • Strengthened roof support structure before installation

Project Impact

  • Reduced household carbon footprint by 40%
